Bus Mall Focus Group

Instructions

  • First imagine you are on a bus.
  • Now imagine you have the kind of disposable income that allows you to make dumb purchases.
  • Once you are in the correct mindset, take a look at the three items in front of you.
  • Click on the item you are most likely to buy in the about scenario.
  • Click continue to confirm your purchase and repeat this process.
  • After you have made 25 selections, you will be given the results.
